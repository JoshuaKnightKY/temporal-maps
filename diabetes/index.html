diabetes by sex over time

Data from the Big Cities Health Coalition (BCHC) from the metopolitan health departments of 15 cities was analyzed, with gaps completed using simple interpolation to display trends in the diabetes mortality rate (deaths per 100k) over time.

Map info

Using the slider bar at the bottom left corner of the map, the user can watch the change in rates. Placing the cursor over the circles will display a tooltip showing specific dates for the year and trends over time.
