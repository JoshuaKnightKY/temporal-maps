School Enrollment in Kenya, 2017

This map shows national data for class enrollment in primary school. County level data is assigned and broken down by gender and class.


Using the slider bar at the bottom left corner of the map, the user can watch the change in rates. Placing the cursor over the circles will display a tooltip showing specific dates for the year and trends over time.

Kenya openData Portal
